# Privacy Policy for TaskPilot: Life Admin
**Last updated: 12/3/2025**

TaskPilot: Life Admin (“the App”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have. TaskPilot is designed to store data locally on your device only, giving you full control over your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Enter Into the App
The App allows you to enter personal information such as:
- Tasks  
- Bills and due dates  
- Calendar events  
- Brain dump notes  
- Streak progress  
- Any text you manually add  

This information is stored only on your device.  
We do not collect, store, upload, or access this data.

### B. App Usage Data
TaskPilot does not collect analytics, tracking data, or usage statistics.

## 2. How Your Information Is Used

All data you enter remains on your device and is used only to provide the App’s core features, such as:
- Displaying tasks and reminders  
- Showing your bills and due dates  
- Managing your calendar  
- Storing brain dump entries  
- Tracking streak progress  

We do not use your information for marketing, analytics, or advertising.

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- Data is stored locally on your device using secure device storage.  
- Your information is never transmitted to us or any third party.  
- You control all data and can delete it at any time by removing the App or clearing the App’s local storage.

## 4. Children’s Privacy

TaskPilot does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data remains on the user’s device, no identifying information is transmitted or stored by us.

## 5. Third-Party Services

TaskPilot does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, APIs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Your data stays completely private.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The “Last updated” date will always reflect the most recent changes.
